Anita Borg Institute : Provides women with tools to develop professional skills in order to attain and retain jobs.
Association for Computing Machinery : Contains a section geared towards women with scholarships and other opportunities to encourage more women to join computer science.
Computing Research Association : Aid in encouraging women to pursue research in computer science.
Women in Technology : Aimed towards advancing women in technology through career development tools and mentoring.
National Center for Women & Information Technology : Meant to establish a community of women in computing both during and after their college experience.
